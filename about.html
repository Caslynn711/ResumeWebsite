About Me

A little bit about me...My name is Cassie and I am a mom to 3 beautiful children. They are my life and everything I do is for them. They definitely bring excitement into my life and challenge me to become my best self. Without them I don't know where I'd be. They saved me. Born and raised in NYC, I moved to Florida at the age of 16. Currently I am in school pursing my Bachelors in Management and Tech Development, with an expected graduation date of June 2025. Along with studying for my Bachelors, I am self studying to become a fullstack software engineer, currently taking Udemy courses. I received a computer programming certificate from St. Petersberg College in 2019. I would say my love of coding began in 2007 during the myspace age. At the time I did not know I was coding, but I remember loving altering the html and css code of my myspace page. When I first began college my goal was to become a physician's assistant, however, I realized I did not love or have a passion for the medical field. Once I realized coding was my actual love, I decided to change majors in 2017. My favorite languages to date are Java (my first love), JavaScript and Python. I'm still on my journey into the tech world, but it has taught me so much about the future and myself in the process that I wouldn't change my path for anything else in the world.
